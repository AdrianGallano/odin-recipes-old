Recipe

"Ribeye Steak"

Ingredients
Ribeye Steak
Salt and Pepper
Olive Oil
Butter
Garlic
Fresh Rosemary and Thyme
Directions
Let Steak Rest: To begin, let the steak rest for 30 minutes to come to room temperature. Salt and pepper to taste.
Prep Skillet and Sear: Next, in a medium sized skillet over
medium high heat add the olive oil. Once it starts to smoke
add the steaks to the skillet. Sear on all of the sides until
they have a golden brown crust.
Add Butter and Herbs: Add the butter and let melt in the skillet.
Add in the garlic and fresh sprigs.
Reduce Heat and Continue Cooking: Reduce the heat to medium and
let them cook until they reach the desired internal temperature. Lastly,
spoon the butter over the steak while they are cooking.
