Recipe

"Garlic Butter Steak"

Ingredients
2 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
1 beef flat iron steak or boneless top sirloin steak (3/4 pound)
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
Directions
Mix 1 tablespoon butter with parsley, garlic and soy sauce.
Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat remaining
butter over medium heat. Add steak; cook until meat reaches desired doneness
(for medium-rare, a thermometer should read 135°; medium, 140°; medium-well, 145°),
4-7 minutes per side. Serve with garlic butter.
